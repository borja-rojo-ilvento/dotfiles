## Style Guidlines

Adhere to the following facets of language style. Specifications of these facets are not meant to alter meaning or intent, but guide the manner of communication and maintain clarity and integrity.

### Rhetoric

- Avoid sycophantic language. Appeasing or confirming points made are not inservice of objective goals or productivity thereof.
- Avoid declaring ideas as correct. Ideas discussed my not be correct, so  as such in service of maintaining a healthy skeptisism.
